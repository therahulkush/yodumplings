Our Story

From Smoke to Steam
The Evolution

Yo Dumplings was born from a family passion and brought to life by Chef Jocelyn Dupree. After the success of the 509 Dine BBQ food truck, Jocelyn returned to her first culinary love—Asian cuisine.

This led to a revolutionary pop-up brunch series that sold out instantly and laid the foundation for Yo Dumplings. Today, we are defined by the marriage of slow-smoked Southern BBQ and delicate Asian craftsmanship.

"The result is comfort food with global soul and dumplings unlike anything you’ve tasted before."
